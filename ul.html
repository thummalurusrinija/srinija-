• Facebook
    • Application: Social networking, staying connected with friends and family, joining interest-based groups, and sharing updates, photos, and videos.
    Features:News Feed, Messenger, Groups, Pages, Events, Marketplace.

  • Instagram
    • Application: Sharing photos and videos, visual storytelling, and following influencers, celebrities, and brands.
    Features:Stories, Reels, IGTV, Direct Messaging, Explore page.

  • Twitter
    • Application: Microblogging, following real-time news, updates from public figures, and engaging in conversations through tweets and hashtags.
    Features:Tweets, Retweets, Likes, Trending Topics, Lists.

  • LinkedIn
    • Application: Professional networking, job searching, and showcasing skills and career achievements.
    Features:Profiles, Connections, Endorsements, Recommendations, Job Listings.

  • WhatsApp
    • Application: Messaging and calling, sharing multimedia, and creating groups for communication.
    Features:Text Messaging, Voice and Video Calls, Status Updates, End-to-End Encryption.

  • Snapchat
    • Application: Sending ephemeral messages and multimedia, engaging with friends through Stories, and using AR filters.
    Features:Snaps, Stories, Lenses, Discover, Chat.
